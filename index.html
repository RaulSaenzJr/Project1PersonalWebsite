About Me

I earned my chef coat from Tom Devlin in 2005 while we were both in Lake Worth, Fl. Over the years I gained valuable experience and worked as a private chef and event planner. In 2016 I became a father and made the decision to change my career path. I pursued an Associates of Applied Science in Computer Information Technology at Austin Community College, graduating in 2023. During my time at ACC, I also pursured a certification as a Registered Behavioral Technician to better support my daughter and other children with autism. Starting in January (2024), I will begin working as an RBT, and I am excited to embark on a new journey as a freelance software developer after graduating from the Software Engineer Bootcamp at the University of Texas at Arlington in March of 2024.
