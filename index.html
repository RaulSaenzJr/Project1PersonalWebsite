About Me

I earned my chef coat from Tom Devlin in 2005 while we were both in Lake Worth, Fl. Over the years I gained valuable experience and worked as a private chef and event planner. In 2021, I began a journey toward an Associates of Applied Science in Computer Information Technology at Austin Community College, and graduated in 2023. During my time at ACC, I also pursued a certification as a Registered Behavioral Technician to better support my daughter and other children with autism. I am currently taking a Software Engineer Bootcamp with the University of Texas at Arlington and QuickStart, Inc.
